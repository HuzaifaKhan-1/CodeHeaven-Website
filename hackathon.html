About Hackathon 2024

Hackathon 2024 is the ultimate challenge for coders. Over the course of 48 hours, you'll collaborate with talented developers, solve complex problems, and push your coding skills to the limit. With a mix of in-person and virtual participation, this hackathon is accessible to everyone.

Event Details

  • Date: Oct 15-17, 2024
  • Location: Virtual & In-Person (San Francisco, New York, London)
  • Registration Fee: $50 (Includes access to all sessions and a swag bag)

Participants will be grouped into teams of 4-6 members. You can either form your own team or be assigned to one. This is an opportunity to meet and work with some of the brightest minds in the industry.
